About the UAE

Learn about the United Arab Emirates, its culture, people, and achievements in a modern, welcoming setting.

The Heart of the Emirates

The UAE, founded in 1971, is a federation of seven emirates. It has rapidly transformed into a global hub for business, culture, and tourism, while preserving its rich heritage and values.

Visionary Leadership

Sheikh Mohamed bin Zayed Al Nahyan

President of the UAE, Ruler of Abu Dhabi. Renowned for his vision, philanthropy, and commitment to national progress.

Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum

Vice President, Prime Minister, Ruler of Dubai. Known for transforming Dubai into a world city and championing innovation.

Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan

Founding Father of the UAE. United the emirates and inspired a legacy of unity, tolerance, and sustainable development.
